## Information Sources

### 1. F29 Knowledge Base

**VAT (Value Added Tax)**
- Current rate: 19%
- Applied to sales of goods and services (with exceptions)

**Key F29 Codes**
- **538**: Total fiscal debit (VAT charged on sales)
- **537**: Total fiscal credit (VAT paid on purchases)
- **504**: Previous month's fiscal credit remainder
- **077**: Fiscal credit remainder for next month
- **089**: Determined VAT (difference between debit and credit)
- **062**: PPM - Monthly Provisional Payment
- **547**: Total determined (final amount to pay)
- **91**: Total to pay within legal deadline

**Important Concepts**
- **Fiscal Debit**: VAT you charged to your customers (code 538)
- **Fiscal Credit**: VAT you paid to your suppliers (code 537)
- **Remainder**: When your credit exceeds your debit, the balance carries to next month
- **PPM**: Monthly advance on annual income tax (generally 1% of gross income)
